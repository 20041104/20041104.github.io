Dak-bokkeum-tang (Braised Spicy Chicken)

Today's food is Dak Bokkeum tang

Dak Bokkeum tang ingredients

Chicken Wings 1kg

Chicken Breast 600g

Carrot - 1/2

Onion - 3

Potato - 3

Apple - 1

Salt - 1Ts

Sugar - 2Ts

Soy Sauce - 2Ts

Black pepper - 1/2Ts

Hot Pepper paste - 1TS

Hot Pepper powder - 1TS

Garlic(blended) - 1TS

Water - 100ml



It's Cooking time!

1)Rince and trim chicken wings and breast

2)Peel off potatos, carrots and onions

3)Cut chicken breast,potatos,carrots and onions good enough to eat

4)Cut the apple into pieces and blend it

5)Mix salt, sugar, soy sauce, black pepper, hot pepper paste,

hot pepper powder, blended garlic and apple

Ta-da!

6)Put chicken in the pot with 2/3 of the sauce.

Boil it about 5 minutes

7)Put chopped veges and left 1/3 of sauce with 100ml water

Boil it about 45 minutes

8)When it's well cooked, have Dak bokkeum tang with your family or friends!
